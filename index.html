About Me

I'm Fernando Pérez Bueno, Ph.D. in Information and Communication Technologies (UGR 2022), Telecommunications Engineer (UGR 2015), and Master Degree in Data Science and Computer Engineering (UGR 2019).

I did my Ph.D. as a Visual Information Processing Group member in the Department of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence (UGR). My research is focused on the use of Bayesian modeling and inference to solve different problems related to image restoration and machine learning. I have experience in medical image (histological and MRI) image enhancement and classification.

Winner of the 2020/2021 3MT UGR (Link to video). On the 3 Minutes Thesis challenge, participants are required to share their thesis in less than 3 minutes, and with only one slide.
